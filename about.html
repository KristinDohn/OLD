ABOUT KRISTIN

Meet Kristin!

Céad míle fáilte!

That means a "hundred thousand welcomes" in Irish Gaelic. I am an American of Irish and Ukrainian descent who has lived in Germany, Italy, and Japan over the last 12 years. I think knowing just a few small facts about someone helps to connect with them in a world where we are so connected that, at times, we appear to not be connected at all.

I have never been to Ireland, and I do not like that fact one bit.

I love a good challenge.

I was raised in the suburbs outside New York City, I am direct, but flexible and reasonable. 

I love coffee.

A lot.

I also love football.

 Life begins after coffee! 

I have an engineering degree from Villanova University but have spent the last 6 years in graphics, web design and social media. That means I think with both sides of my brain, I am a great blend of technical meets artistic. I can be a lone wolf and a bee, thriving with the hive. I can be serious and fun.

I love humor and have many moods, check some of them out on the right!

I am open minded and accepting of all walks of life.

I was in the army. (HOOAH EOD!) I do not expect any special treatment for that. It was brief. I only tell you so that you know I can take direction, or lead.

You can connect with me in many ways. I'd love to chat with you! What could be better than some real interaction? Thanks to today's technology, digital interaction can be pretty close to the real thing! Feel free to email me, request a phone call, or face time.
