PROJECT DETAIL

LinguaFest

LinguaFest was a week long intensive language immersion event for high school students. The event director requested a branded design that could be used each year on T-Shirts, business cards, event marketing and any other materials.

The event included students in ten different linguistic courses, including those taking English as a Second Language. Students signed a commitment to speak nothing but their intended target language throughout the duration of the week long event held in a different country each year. The logo is comprised of a chat bubble, with "Speak!" written in each of the target languages offered.
