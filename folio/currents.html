PROJECT DETAIL

"Currents" Internal Newsletter

DoDEA Pacific Currents was a quarterly newsletter designed to disseminate good news and information to staff throughout an Asia-based military organization. I branded the newsletter with a water styled header and utilized bright colors throughout to appeal to the grade school atmosphere. For the inaugural edition I utilized a photo cube to snap images of a laptop being used in a student 1:1 Laptop initiative. I used an angled photo to highlight the opening article and a composite on the second page to highlight laptop features. The composite was my idea of creatively highlighting the laptop by compositing various position of the laptop screen into one image.

2014 NSPRA Print Newsletter Merit Award!
